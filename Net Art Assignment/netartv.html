After the destruction of his home, the little pig fled the wreckage and ran all the way to his bretheren's house that was made of stick. Shortly after seeking refuge, the Big Bad Wolf approched the house made of sticks and let out the same demands he did at the previous house. The pigs once again refused to let him enter, feeling certain the apex predator wouldn't be able to get in. However, the dexterous swine was mistaken.
